About Me

I have my B.A. Psychology with an emphasis in Clinical Psychology and a Masters in Business Administration with an emphasis in Finance. I am 27 years old. Happily married with a passion for serving God!

I have a new passion and that is coding. And, to be honest- I didn't think I was going to like it and even though it's stressful- I really enjoy my time coding. I changed my career because I want to provide for my family. I am looking for financial independence and freedom from debt.
